Yes, exactly! The data displayed is what scientists care about most for cell culture bioprocessing:

Critical Parameters (what we're showing):

pH (6.9-7.4): Cell viability - too high/low kills cells

pO2 (dissolved oxygen): Cell respiration - need 20-80% saturation

pCO2 (carbon dioxide): Metabolic waste - high = stress

Glucose (g/L): Cell food - monitor consumption rate

Lactate (g/L): Metabolic waste - high = poor cell health

Why This Matters:

Scientists monitor these 5 parameters every 4-8 hours during production

Out-of-range values = batch failure = millions of dollars lost

Nova FLEX2 measures all 5 in 60 seconds from one sample
