 Implementation Strategy
Confidence-Based Processing:
High Confidence (>95%) - Automatic:

Known instrument formats with perfect parsing

Standard file structures with complete metadata

Previously validated similar conversions

Action: Automatic ASM generation and certification

Medium Confidence (80-95%) - Flagged Review:

Minor format variations or missing metadata

New instrument models from known vendors

Unusual but parseable data structures

Action: Generate ASM, flag for batch review

Low Confidence (<80%) - Human Validation:

Unknown formats or parsing failures

Corrupted or incomplete data files

Critical quality issues detected

Action: Queue for human expert review

Scalability Benefits:
Volume Handling:

80-90% automatic processing for standard formats

5-15% flagged review (batch processing)

5% human validation (exception handling)

Quality Assurance:

ML-powered confidence scoring improves over time

Continuous learning from human corrections

Automated quality metrics for all conversions
